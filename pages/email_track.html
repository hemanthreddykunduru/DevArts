Email Tracking Dashboard

Smart Email Analytics

A zero-cost, AI-powered email tracking solution that provides real-time analytics and insights into email campaign performance. Built with modern web technologies and intelligent automation.

Why This Project?

Traditional email tracking tools charge premium fees for basic analytics. This dashboard democratizes email tracking by offering enterprise-level features at zero cost, leveraging free-tier services and open-source technologies.

AI Integration Points

AI enhances the dashboard through intelligent pattern recognition and predictive analytics:

  • → Smart Categorization: AI automatically classifies email types and recipient behavior
  • → Anomaly Detection: Machine learning identifies unusual open/click patterns
  • → Optimal Send Time: AI predicts best sending times based on historical engagement
  • → Subject Line Analysis: Natural language processing scores subject effectiveness
