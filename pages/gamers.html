Echoes of the Pharaohs

Step into a world of ancient Egypt, filled with adventure, mystery, and AI-based warfare. Explore vast deserts, uncover forgotten treasures, and engage in strategic battles to restore peace to a land in turmoil. Built with Unreal Engine 5 over 7 months of intense development.

Development Journey

Engine: Unreal Engine 5 - Leveraging Nanite virtualized geometry, Lumen global illumination, and advanced physics simulation for unprecedented visual fidelity and realistic environmental interactions.

Development Time: 7 Months of dedicated development, including concept design, asset creation, gameplay mechanics implementation, AI behavior programming, and extensive playtesting.

Why Unreal Engine 5? UE5's cutting-edge rendering capabilities allow for film-quality visuals in real-time, photorealistic lighting with Lumen, seamless open-world streaming with World Partition, and advanced AI systems through Behavior Trees and Environment Query System (EQS).

Core Game Features

◆ Advanced Climbing & Motion Warping

Experience true physical interaction with procedural climbing system. Scale pyramids, cliff faces, and ancient structures with realistic hand-placement and dynamic animation blending. Motion warping ensures smooth transitions between ledges.

◆ Intelligent AI Combat System

Engage with adaptive AI enemies using Unreal Engine 5's Behavior Tree system. Distract enemies with throwable objects, utilize stealth mechanics, or engage in direct combat with swords, bows, spears, shields, and magical artifacts. Enemies react to player tactics and adapt their strategies.

◆ Diverse Animals & Mount System

Ride and tame majestic desert creatures including camels for speed, rhinos for power, and elephants for warfare. Each mount has unique abilities and characteristics. Hunt wildlife for resources or observe natural ecosystem behaviors.

◆ Dynamic Day-Night & Weather System

A fully dynamic 24/7 real-time sun cycle powered by Lumen global illumination. Watch as shadows shift across ancient monuments, experience temperature changes affecting gameplay, and witness sandstorms that alter visibility and combat effectiveness. Night brings different enemy types and challenges.

◆ Loyal Pet Companion System

Bond with your loyal fox companion that assists in exploration and combat, or summon your crow ally when stamina is depleted to scout ahead and mark enemies. Pets level up alongside you and learn new abilities.

◆ Parkour & Free-Running

Navigate the world with fluid parkour mechanics including wall-running, vaulting, sliding, and contextual jumping. Chain movements together for spectacular traversal sequences through ancient cities and desert landscapes.

◆ Deep Crafting & Upgrade System

Collect resources from defeated enemies and the environment to craft weapons, armor, potions, and tools. Upgrade your gear with ancient Egyptian enchantments and rare materials found in hidden tombs.

◆ Puzzle-Filled Ancient Tombs

Explore intricate tomb dungeons featuring environmental puzzles, pressure plates, moving platforms, hieroglyphic riddles, and deadly traps. Uncover legendary treasures and powerful artifacts hidden within.

◆ Epic Boss Battles

Face off against massive boss enemies including giant scorpions, sand serpents, cursed pharaohs, and mythological creatures. Each boss requires unique strategies and offers legendary rewards upon defeat.

◆ Branching Story & Choices

Make meaningful decisions that affect the game's narrative outcome. Choose between honor and power, mercy and vengeance. Your choices determine which factions support you and influence the game's multiple endings.
